Email subjects are static strings that do no marker replacement (with few exceptions that use the %name). Please enter the subject exactly as it should appear in the email.

Email templates are written in Fluid html. This allows for access to data values (within curly brackets i.e.{settings.pid.storage}) and certain helper tags (starting with <f:). Be careful with this extra information. A misspelling, missing bracket, or a missing quotation can throw an error and prevent the email from being sent.
